Welcome to my photography website. I created this as a one-stop area where I can access my most beautiful memories as well as show others my vision of the world. Often-times, I see a lack of saturation and color in the pictures, as if the world others see the world as it is. I hate to think like that and often find myself adding color and presenting my vision in the best possible light. Of course my color grading skills and resources are limited, but I make due with what I have and still create whatever memories I have to the best of my ability.

歡迎來到我的攝影網站。我創建這個網站作為一個集中區域，讓我能夠隨時訪問我最美好的回憶，同時向他人展示我對世界的視角。我常常發現很多照片缺乏飽和度 和色彩，彷彿別人所見的世界就是它本身。我不想這樣思考，經常會添加色彩，並以最佳的方式呈現我的視角。當然，我的色彩調整技巧和資源有限，但我會充分利 用我所擁有的，讓我的每個回憶都能展現得最好。
